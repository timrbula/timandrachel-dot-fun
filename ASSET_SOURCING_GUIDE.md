## ğŸµ MIDI Music Files

### Where to Find MIDI Files

**Free MIDI Libraries:**

- **FreeMIDI.org** (https://freemidi.org/)
- **MIDI World** (https://www.midiworld.com/)
- **BitMIDI** (https://bitmidi.com/)
- **Classical Archives** (https://www.classicalarchives.com/)

### Recommended MIDI Files

#### Wedding Classics

- [ ] **Wedding March** (Mendelssohn) - "Here Comes the Bride"
  - Search: "wedding march midi" or "bridal chorus midi"
  - Duration: 2-3 minutes
- [ ] **Canon in D** (Pachelbel)
  - Search: "canon in d midi" or "pachelbel midi"
  - Duration: 3-4 minutes
- [ ] **Bridal Chorus** (Wagner)
  - Search: "bridal chorus midi" or "wagner wedding midi"
  - Duration: 2-3 minutes

#### NYC-Themed Options

- [ ] **New York, New York** (Frank Sinatra)
  - Search: "new york new york midi"
  - Style: Upbeat, celebratory
- [ ] **Empire State of Mind** (Jay-Z/Alicia Keys)
  - Search: "empire state of mind midi"
  - Note: May be harder to find quality version
- [ ] **Theme from New York, New York** (Liza Minnelli)
  - Search: "theme from new york midi"
  - Classic NYC anthem

#### Fun 90s Alternatives

- [ ] **90s pop hits** in MIDI format
  - Search: "90s pop midi" or specific song names
  - Examples: "I Want It That Way", "Wannabe", etc.

### MIDI File Requirements

- **Format:** .mid or .midi
- **Size:** Under 100KB (most MIDI files are small)
- **Quality:** Test playback in multiple browsers
- **Length:** 2-4 minutes (can loop)
- **Compatibility:** Ensure works with Web Audio API

### Creating Custom MIDI

If you can't find the perfect file:

- **MuseScore** (https://musescore.org/) - Free music notation software
- **GarageBand** (Mac) - Export as MIDI
- **Online MIDI Editor** (https://signal.vercel.app/edit) - Browser-based
