## Neighborhood Guide

### Greenpoint, Brooklyn

**Character:** Hip, artistic neighborhood with Polish heritage, waterfront views, and trendy restaurants/bars

**Things to Do:**

- Transmitter Park (waterfront park with Manhattan views)
- McCarren Park
- Greenpoint Beer & Ale vintage bar
- Local coffee shops and bakeries
- Polish restaurants and delis

**Food & Drink:**

- Five Leaves (brunch spot)
- Paulie Gee's (pizza)
- Achilles Heel (cocktail bar)
- Peter Pan Donut & Pastry Shop
- Variety Coffee Roasters

### Getting Around

**To/From Airports:**

- **JFK:** AirTrain to Jamaica, E train to Court Sq, G train to Greenpoint Ave (~90 min)
- **LaGuardia:** Q70 bus to Jackson Heights, 7 train to Court Sq, G train to Greenpoint Ave (~75 min)
- **Newark:** NJ Transit to Penn Station, transfer to subway (~90 min)
- **Taxi/Uber:** $60-100 depending on airport and traffic

**Within Brooklyn:**

- G train connects to Williamsburg, Park Slope, Carroll Gardens
- L train at Bedford Ave connects to Manhattan (14th St)
- Citi Bike stations throughout neighborhood
